Kiara

Kiara Khanna was born on 7 August 2017 in Hyderabad, Telangana. Kiara has a sister name Myra Khana. Kiara and Myra both are twins. They both make acting videos on Instagram where they have more than 300K followers. Kiara has appeared in many movies like Thankgod, Bandasingh, Baramulla and Sambahadur. In December 2023, Kiara grabbed headlines for featuring in the romantic family drama film Hi Nana as Mahi along with Mrunal Thakur and Ghanta Naveen Babu.
